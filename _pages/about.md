<h2 style="color:#500000;">Vision</h2>
<div style="text-align: justify; font-family: Calibri, Arial, sans-serif; line-height:1.8; font-size:13pt; color:#111;">
<p>Inspired by Stephen Hawking's principle that <i style="color:#500000;">"One of the basic rules of the universe is that nothing is perfect,"</i> we need to embrace imperfection to innovate and improve through engineering, constantly seeking better, adaptable, and sustainable solutions.</p>
<p>My vision is to advance <b style="color:#500000;">knowledge-grounded</b> and <b style="color:#500000;">AI-infused</b> transportation systems that integrate <b style="color:#500000;">cyber</b>, <b style="color:#500000;">physical</b>, and <b style="color:#500000;">human</b> dimensions to enhance <b style="color:#500000;">safety</b>, <b style="color:#500000;">stability</b>, <b style="color:#500000;">intelligence</b>, and <b style="color:#500000;">resilience</b> in future mobility networks.</p>
</div>
