<h2 style="color:#500000;">Vision</h2>
<div style="text-align: justify; font-family: Calibri, Arial, sans-serif; line-height:1.7; font-size:11pt; color:#111;">
<p>Inspired by Stephen Hawking's principle that <i>"One of the basic rules of the universe is that nothing is perfect,"</i> we need to embrace imperfection to innovate and improve through engineering, constantly seeking better, adaptable, and sustainable solutions.</p>
<p>My vision is to advance <b>knowledge-grounded</b> and <b>AI-infused</b> transportation systems that integrate <b>cyber</b>, <b>physical</b>, and <b>human</b> dimensions to enhance <b>safety</b>, <b>stability</b>, <b>intelligence</b>, and <b>resilience</b> in future mobility networks.</p>
</div>

<h2 style="color:#500000;">Biography</h2>
