<h2 style="color:#500000;">Research Overview</h2>

<p>
My research aims to advance <b style="color:#500000;">knowledge-grounded</b> and <b style="color:#500000;">AI-infused</b> transportation systems that remain <b style="color:#500000;">safe</b>, <b style="color:#500000;">stable</b>, and <b style="color:#500000;">resilient</b> across <b style="color:#500000;">cyber-physical-human</b> domains.  
I combine theoretical modeling, multimodal learning, and generative AI to understand and mitigate disturbances that arise from automation dynamics, cyber vulnerabilities, and human variability.  
This approach bridges transportation science with artificial intelligence by linking <i>traffic flow theory</i> and <i>control modeling</i> with <i>large language models</i> and <i>digital twins</i> to enable resilient mobility.
</p>
